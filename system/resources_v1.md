## 💡 Tips for Using These Resources

### For System Design Mastery:
1. **Start with fundamentals** - Read High Scalability Blog and ByteByteGo for foundational concepts
2. **Study real architectures** - Follow Netflix, Uber, and Airbnb engineering blogs for real-world case studies
3. **Practice trade-offs** - Every design decision has pros/cons; focus on understanding why, not just what
4. **Build mental models** - Compare how different companies solve similar problems

### For Principal Engineer Career:
1. **Understand the role** - Read Staff Engineer by Will Larson and The Staff Engineer's Path
2. **Build influence** - Focus on cross-team impact and technical strategy
3. **Join communities** - Rands Leadership Slack and LeadDev for networking
4. **Practice writing** - Document your architectural decisions and learnings

### For Interview Preparation:
1. **Master the framework** - Use a structured approach (requirements → estimation → design → optimize)
2. **Practice out loud** - Use Interviewing.io or Exponent for mock interviews
3. **Know your patterns** - Load balancing, caching, sharding, consistency models
4. **Study company-specific** - Check LeetCode Discuss for target company questions
